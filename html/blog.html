Tech stocks surge as earnings beat expectations

Several technology companies reported better-than-expected earnings in their most recent quarterly reports, leading to a surge in the stock market. Major players such as Apple, Amazon, and Microsoft all exceeded analysts' estimates, and investors responded by buying up shares in these companies. This resulted in a boost to the overall tech sector, with the Nasdaq index reaching a new all-time high. Experts attribute the strong performance to increased demand for technology products and services due to the ongoing shift towards remote work and online shopping during the pandemic.
